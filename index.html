Gerald's Great Escape from Alcatraz

You wake up in a prison cell in Alcatraz for beating Jesus in a game of pong. What do you do?

You found a poster with a hidden hole in it to escape, also a moveable toilet with passage below it.

Congratulations you have just escaped Alcatraz and you have no consequences

You crawl through the hole behind the poster, while moving you see a guard above you posing menacingly to something

You get spotted, arrested and brought back. What did you think was going to happen?

You keep moving through the hole towards a dead end and the ground beneath you opens and you fall into hell. Guess the person who dug the hole suffered the same fate.

You got shanked by a random prisoner and died in your sleep. Good job!
